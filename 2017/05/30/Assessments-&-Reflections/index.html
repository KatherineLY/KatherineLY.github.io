Created at 2017-05-30 Updated at 2017-06-08 Category Assessment & Reflection

1. Group Presentation

Assignment link

http://or6ltz062.bkt.clouddn.com/learner%20beliefs.pdf
It was the first time that we learned,analyzed and presented a part of knowledge point by ourselves. Our assignment was to analyze the learner beliefs in the second language learning. Since the assingment was a group work, we divided every part of work clearly. But before we starting to make the PPT, our group members discussed every important points together. We were trying to sort out every relevant detail in the material. Although we had some problems and divergence like different understandings in the same context or different ideas about the style of PPT, we solved them by checking more convincing academic proofs and making more communication. And after settling down all the outline and content that we were going to talk, we practiced our speed in the presentation and designed every part of the flash to meet the time requirement.
From this group presentation assignment, I learned that it is a good habit to collect as much relevant study materials as you can, and the methods of searching materials should be various. Because you will never know when you are going to use them, especislly in an urgency. Moreover, everyone has the ability of self-study, it is terrible that we are always waiting for someone else to give us something, we are supposed to gain by ourselves.

2. Annotated Bibliography

Assignment link

http://or6ltz062.bkt.clouddn.com/%E6%9E%97%E6%82%A0AB.pdf
As for me, the main point of this assignment is to train our ability of generalization. To be honest, I didn’t like to read some academic articles or magazines before, they were too far from my life. And it seemed they have no business to do with me. But I had to finish the assignment. It was really difficult to settle down the topic of my annotated bibliography at the first, and I had no idea what I was doing at that time. But after dicussing with classmates, I started my work. The principle of the topic is to be specific. So, after searching many relevant materials about second language learning, I decided to choose the Factors Affecting Incidental Vocabulary Acquisition in English Reading as for my topic. I choose this topic because I heard about incidental vocabulary acquisition before, it was a very good chance to learn more about it.
I was quite satisfied with my generalization of the two academic articles, but I neglected the layout of my annotated bibliography. I was a little sad when I saw my score, but i am grateful that I have the chance to realize my shortcoming that I always focus on the content, now I know the layout is also very important. And through this assingemnt, I also learned a lot of websites that provide academic information. It seems that I am closer to the academic things.

3. Literature Review

Assignment link

http://or6ltz062.bkt.clouddn.com/Incidental%20Vocabulary%20Acquisition%20in%20Reading.pdf
Literature review is the extension of the annotated bibliography, so I finished it quickly. I was afraid that I would not have enough time to searching relevant materials, so when I was working on the annotated bibliography, I prepared materials for the literature review already. What I had to do is just sort out all the useful information in the materials and generalized them with my own understanding.
I learned how to manage my time in this assignment, and the early prepared materials really improved my efficiency. I love the way that every thing that I need is in an order. And I am sure my disordered study schedule won’t appear anymore.
