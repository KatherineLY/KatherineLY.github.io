Conclusion

Created at 2017-05-30 Updated at 2017-06-08 Category Conclusion

Here, I would like to make brief conclusion in two aspects.
First, after learning the Language Learning Theory in this semester, I know some knowledge about the language learning, like there are developmental sequences and critical period in the language learning. The environment of language, the influence of first language and learners’ personality are also important factors in the second language acquisition. And after I learning the language learning in early childhood, I pay more attention to babies’ language learning whenever I met one in daily life. And I also like to share what I’ve learned from this book with mothers. In addition, the researches and analysis in this book are very practical for education. If I became an English teacher one day, the knowledge that I learned in this book would help me to analyze and recognize some ordinary problem that I might had in my teaching.
Secondly, I want to talk about all assignments that I did in this semester, each assignment seemed a challenge for me at the first, but when I review them now, they made me more experienced and careful. It was a busy semester, but only busy and substantial life can be meaningful.
